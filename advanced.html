Kalimeris

Kalimeris is a genus of plants in the family Asteraceae. It was first described in 1825 by the French botanist Alexandre Henri Gabriel de Cassini. This genus occurs mainly in eastern Asia, but is also naturalised on Hawaii. They can grow to a height of 1-1.5 m. The foliage is herbaceous.

Oemleria

A shrub commonly known as osoberry or Indian plum, is the sole species in genus Oemleria. Native to the Pacific coast and ranges of North America, from British Columbia, Canada to Santa Barbara County, California, U.S.A., it is among the first plants to leaf out and flowers early in the spring.

Ratibida

Ratibida is a genus of North American plants in the tribe Heliantheae within the family Asteraceae. Members of the genus are commonly known as prairie coneflowers or mexican-hat.
